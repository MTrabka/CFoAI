Welcome to the Inverted Turing Test!

You are participating in an experiment where an AI will act as the judge. The goal of this test is to determine whether the AI can distinguish whether it is conversing with a human or another AI based on the responses it receives.

  • Your answers are anonymous: Your responses will not be personally identifiable.
  • Collective analysis: Your answers will be analyzed as part of a larger group for research purposes.
  • Answer honestly and thoughtfully: There are no right or wrong answers. The AI will ask you questions, and we are interested in how you respond to them.
  • Responses are not evaluated: The content of the answers is not judged, but the focus is on how well the AI interacts and determines whether the participant is human or AI.

The AI will begin the conversation by asking you questions. Please continue answering as you would in a normal conversation.

Thank you for participating, and we hope you enjoy the experience!
